## Human Summary

Revision 4 has five evidence-backed material risks: wrong-source assembly, stale-authority exposure, corrupt replay authority, envelope/result boundary escape, and wrong trusted-contract dispatch. The Design prevents or fails closed on each risk within CAP-D2 and supplies explicit, safely sequenced currentness, recovery, and exposure-fencing obligations to `workflowd-vs3.4.3`, `workflowd-vs3.4.4`, and `workflowd-vs3.4.7`; every obligation has a deterministic verification target. Residual effects are bounded to rejected or delayed work rather than execution under substituted authority, so the Design is ready for the human Design gate without accepting a material residual risk.
