# Design Boundary Review

## Verdict
`ScopeClean`

## Human Summary
The Design keeps CAP-D2 ownership on the six typed contracts, exact source assembly, catalog dispatch, bounded validation, and exact request replay identity. Its references to stage state, production, publication, progression, Design-owner effects, and integrated verification are limited to typed inputs or outputs needed by the contracts, while the named downstream Beads retain their lifecycles and effects. Keep the Design as written.
