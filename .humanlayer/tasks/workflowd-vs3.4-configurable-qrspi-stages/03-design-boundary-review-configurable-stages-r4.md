# Design Boundary Review

## Verdict
`ScopeClean`

## Human Summary
Revision 4 keeps the configured stage runtime, publication-scoped reconciliation, durable local owner handoffs, and legacy upgrade path inside `workflowd-vs3.4`, while leaving review, gate, Provenance, route-policy, presentation, operational-status, readiness, and capacity lifecycles with their stated owners or outside current authority. Directly queryable recovery fields are required internal support, not a status product, and the cumulative-storage concern is recorded as an unaccepted residual risk without adding an unauthorized control. No blocking finding IDs were found; keep the Design as written for semantic-boundary purposes.
